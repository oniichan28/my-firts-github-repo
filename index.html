About Sylhet

Sylhet, known as the "City of Saints" and for its natural beauty, offers diverse attractions for tourists. It's famous for its lush tea gardens, rolling hills, and historical sites like the mausoleum of Hazrat Shah Jalal. Visitors can explore the scenic Jaflong area with its river and mountains, or enjoy the serene Ratargul Swamp Forest. The city also boasts landmarks like the Ali Amjad Clock Tower and the historic Shah Jalal Dargah.

Ratargul Swamp Forest

Known as the Amazon of Bangladesh, Ratargul is a unique freshwater swamp forest. Perfect for boat rides during monsoon.

Jaflong

A picturesque location on the border of Meghalaya, India, known for stone collection, crystal clear rivers, and views of the Khasi mountains.

Srimangal

The tea capital of Bangladesh. Explore miles of lush tea gardens, the famous 7-layer tea, and peaceful hills.

Bisnakandi

A hidden gem with clear waters, colorful stones, and hills that touch the sky. Perfect for nature lovers and adventurers.
